Udemy

Udemy is like an online library with all sorts of resources waiting for you dig into and learn. I discovered Udemy some time after my time on Team Treehouse and Udemy was so much more. The courses are expensive but they go on sale a lot throughout the year. If a course I am interested is $100 one day, it could be on sale for between $9.99 and $13.99 soon.

With Udemy, I can find multiple sources on a certain subject that I am wanting to learn and be able to read reviews for that particular course and make a decision on whether or not it is worth it. With Udemy, I find myself starting multiple new courses but primarily to get the information I am needing to learn and moving on with my next task.
